How will you use this information?

We respect your privacy and do not tolerate spam and will never sell, rent, lease or give away your information (name, address, email, etc.) to any third party. Nor will we send you unsolicited email. If you choose to provide your cell phone number and/or email address, then we will send you notifications relating to your child, classroom activities, or important notifications (i.e. weather closings). You will have the option to opt out of notifications at any time.

What should I include?

Household income should include earnings from work (before deductions), child support or alimony, payments received from pensions, retirement, and social security, or any other income for all household members. Please include total yearly income for all household sources, and total number of household members.
